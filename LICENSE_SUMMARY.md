## Common Questions

### Q: Can people still view my code on GitHub?
**A:** Yes, but they can't use the patterns commercially without permission.

### Q: Do I need to register this license?
**A:** No, it's automatically valid. Registration is optional for extra legal benefits.

### Q: What if someone forks my repo?
**A:** They can fork for learning, but using patterns commercially violates the license.

### Q: Can I change the license later?
**A:** Yes, you own it. You can update terms anytime (applies to new versions).

### Q: What about contributions?
**A:** You should create a CLA (Contributor License Agreement) if you accept PRs.

### Q: How do I price licenses?
